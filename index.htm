Athens, Greece

A Comprehensive Travel Guide

Athens in a Nutshell

The capital city of Greece. A city at the crossroads between modernity and antiquity, Athens stands alone as a city with just as much culture as it boasts fun. With plenty of good food, warm weather, and educational sites, Athens has a little bit for everyone. Come explore with us!

Attractions

Parthenon

An ancient temple dedicated to the Greek Goddess Athena, the namesake of the city. The most known site in Athens.

Gyro

A Gyro is a traditional Greek dish served with pork, feta cheese, tomatoes, and a yogurt sauce all wrapped in a pita.

Acropolis

An ancient citadel with its own museum filled with culture and history. A must-see for any first timer.

Syntagma Square

The city center filled with famous Greek architecture and cuisine. A cultural hub for any tourist.

Spotlight

ο Θανάσης Restaurant

What's the first thing you do when you visit a new city? Eat, of course. Our team of experts chose "O Thanasis" kebab restaraunt as the pinnacle of all Athenian food. Fill up on traditional Greek gyros, salads, and seafood for dinner, but make sure you leave some room for dessert. O Thanasis is also famous for its Greek pasrties like Δίπλες (pronounced as "thiples"), a fried dough variant, and μελομακάρονο (pronounced melomakarona), a round cookie with honey and walnuts.
